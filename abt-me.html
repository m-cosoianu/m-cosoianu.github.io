Higher Lower

Higher Lower is a game where you play against the computer and once it throws the dice you have to guess if it is higher or lower than what you will throw. You gain and lose points. The game stops once either you or the computer run out of points.

Chess

Chess is a strategic board game played on a 64-square grid where two players move 16 pieces each to capture the opponent's king. The game requires foresight and tactical planning, with the objective being to checkmate the opponent's king.

Block Blast!

Block Blast is a puzzle game where players strategically place blocks of various shapes onto a grid to create and clear complete lines, earning points. The challenge lies in fitting the blocks efficiently to avoid filling up the grid, which would end the game.
